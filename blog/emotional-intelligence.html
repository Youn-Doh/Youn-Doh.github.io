Emotional Intelligence Exploration

  • What is Emotional Intelligence?

    Emotional Intelligence is a skill that you can practice yourself with having a recognition and control your emotions. 'Dr. Goleman described emotional intelligence as a person's ability to manage their feelings so that those feelings are expressed appropriately and effectively.'


  • How is EQ different to IQ?

    IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a measure of ability to think logically and EQ (Emotional Intelligence Quotient) measur of ability to manage and cotrol emotions.


  • Why is EQ important?

    EQ is important because knowing your status of emotions helps to unerstand myself and other people. We live in 'changeable', 'uncertain', 'complex' and 'ambiguous' world, to survive knowing and understanding each other is significant to achieve key objectives in our lives.
